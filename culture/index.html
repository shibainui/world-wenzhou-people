舌尖上的乡愁

Taste of Home

温州糯米饭、鱼丸汤、灯盏糕、炒粉干——这些地道的温州味道，是全球温州人心中最温暖的记忆。无论巴黎还是纽约，一碗糯米饭就是故乡。

Sticky rice, fish ball soup, lantern cakes, stir-fried rice noodles — wherever Wenzhou people go, these flavors define home.

温州方言

The Wenzhou Dialect

温州话被誉为"中国最难懂的方言"之一，保留了大量古汉语的音韵特征。在海外温州人社区中，温州话依然是家庭和商会中的主要交流语言。

Known as one of China's most difficult dialects, Wenzhouhua preserves ancient phonological features. It remains the primary language in diaspora homes and business associations.

民间艺术

Folk Arts & Traditions

温州鼓词、瓯剧、瓯绣——这些独特的民间艺术承载着温州人的文化记忆。即便远在海外，每逢重要节庆，温州社区依然会组织鼓词表演和传统戏曲演出，让年轻一代感受故乡的文化根脉。

Wenzhou drum ballads, Ou Opera, and Ou embroidery carry the community's cultural memory. Even abroad, Wenzhou communities organize traditional performances during festivals to keep their heritage alive for the next generation.
